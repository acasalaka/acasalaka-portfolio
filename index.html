A little about me,

I am someone who thrives on curiosity and growth. Whether it’s diving deep into front-end development or managing IT projects, I’m always drawn to spaces where I can build, improve, and collaborate. My journey in tech has been shaped by a love for design, structure, and problem-solving, which naturally led me to explore front-end web development and project management. I find joy in crafting products that not only work well, but also feel intuitive and human-centered.

Outside of tech, a big part of me has always gravitated toward organizing events. It’s been part of my life for nearly a decade. Recently, I’ve served as a liaison officer at Java Jazz Festival 2025 and talent coordinator for Musikal Petualangan Sherina 2025, where it taught me the magic of connecting people through shared creativity.

Whether I'm writing lines of code or building an event to life, both worlds challenge me to think critically, adapt quickly, and solve real problems (just in a slightly different format). At the end of the day, there’s nothing more exciting to me than being part of a team that turns ideas into impact, whether it happens on a screen or on a stage!

Selected Works

SiSPA

Full-Stack Web Developer and UI/UX Designer

SiSPA is an internal information system specifically designed to support and simplify the operational processes of PT Salaka Pranata Adicara. This system includes managing client data, vendors, inventory, finance, and event documentation to be more efficient, structured, and integrated. Through features such as account management, inventory, cash flow, vendors, clients, to creating POs, invoices and final reports, SiSPA helps companies maintain data integrity and accelerate operational processes. Project Link.

PathFinder

UI/UX Designer

PathFinder is a mobile-based application that provides a platform for high school students to find complete information about colleges and appropriate majors, but also find mentors to help with the process. This application also supports students to find suitable careers and available job vacancies with the help of mentors who can provide direct guidance to students. Project Link.

UlasBuku

Full-Stack Developer

UlasBuku is a web-based application that allows users to provide reviews on books they have read and read reviews of books they want to read. This application allows users to share their reading experiences and provide useful information to others who want to read the book. Along with communicating through a forum page and dedicated messenger platform with other users. Project Link.

Sistem Periodik Unsur

Front-End Web Developer and UI/UX Designer

This project was made when I was a senior in high school. It was made as a part of the high school completion requirement. Website Sistem Periodik Unsur is a website that can be used to access information about the elements in a periodic table. This was aimed to be informational and also interactive, hence why the users can hover over each buttons and find facts about each elements. Users can also access the downloadable PDF for them to use. Project Link.
